The mixtape is Live's introduction to the hip-hop Industry. Live raps about the industry's rules and conventions that most new South African artists have to go through, while striving for the possibility of being recognised.

These songs describe the inevitable harsh realities that life brings, and that Live has to face as a person and as an artist, such as death, fear and doubts. In this mixtape we also learn that Live has a strong Christian background and there are Bible and poetry references that he uses to depict his stories.
